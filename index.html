How to Use This Exercise

A quick reference for facilitators and participants — share this before your session.

🧭 Facilitator Steps

  1. 1.Open this tool and click "I'm the Facilitator." Enter the family name and your name.
  2. 2.A session code is created (e.g. COBBFAMILY-2026). Share it with participants before you start.
  3. 3.Participants open the same link, click "I'm a Participant," enter the code and their name.
  4. 4.Guide Part 1 — everyone fills their brain dump on their own device. When done, click Reveal All Answers.
  5. 5.Move to Part 2. All answers appear in the shared sidebar. Pick a template and shape the draft together as a group.
  6. 6.Save drafts. Try all three templates. Head to Part 3 to compare them side by side.

👥 Participant Steps

  1. 1.Open the link your facilitator shared and click "I'm a Participant."
  2. 2.Enter the session code and your name.
  3. 3.Fill in all four brain dump quadrants with your honest, unedited ideas.
  4. 4.Click Submit My Answers when you're done.
  5. 5.Once the facilitator reveals answers, you'll see everyone's responses pooled together.
  6. 6.Join the conversation about which draft sounds most like your family.
About Session Codes: Each family gets a unique code (e.g. COBBFAMILY-2026). The facilitator creates it — participants use it to join. Sessions are stored in your browser, so the facilitator should use the same browser/device for the whole session. If participants need to reconnect, they rejoin with the same code and name.
Remember: This is a draft, not an engraving. A mission statement earns its permanence by being tested in real decisions. Every request that comes to your fund travels through this statement. Does it align? Yes — keep talking. No — easy pass, no guilt required. That permission to say no is one of the greatest gifts your mission statement gives you.
