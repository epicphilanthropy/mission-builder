THE PROBLEM
What keeps you up at night?
Think about:
What breaks your heart about the world?
What injustice or gap do you see that others might miss?
If you could wave a magic wand, what would you fix?
What problem are you tired of seeing go unsolved?
What's the thing that makes you say "someone needs to do something about this"?
What issue do you find yourself talking about at dinner parties?
💙
YOUR WHY
Why do you care about this?
Think about:
What's your personal connection to this issue?
What life experience shaped this passion?
Why you? Why your family?
When did you first realize this mattered to you?
What would be different in your life if this problem didn't exist?
Who in your life has been affected by this?
🎯
YOUR FOCUS
What/who will you support?
Think about:
Who or what will you invest in?
What kind of work resonates with you?
Where do you think your money can make the biggest difference?
What approach feels most aligned with your values?
Are you drawn to direct service, policy change, or something else?
What organizations or efforts inspire you?
🌱
THE IMPACT/CHANGE
What change do you want to see in the world?
Think about:
What does a better world look like to you?
If your giving works, what's different?
What transformation are you hoping to create?
Paint a picture: what changes?
Who benefits and how?
What does "better" look like for this issue?

When you're ready, move to the next step to build your mission statement.
